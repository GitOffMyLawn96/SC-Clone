# Starcopter Content Audit

## Current Core Message
Starcopter positions `HIGHDRA` as a new-generation high-performance drone platform that combines long real-world flight time, heavy payload support, modularity, and flexible commercial access through a pay-per-use model.

## Strongest Messaging Themes
- Access to high-end data without the traditional ownership burden
- Real flight time, not lab-only benchmark claims
- High payload capacity with modular payload integration
- `Auterion`-powered platform credibility
- Early `C3` certification and compliance readiness
- Support for `LiDAR`, `RGB`, `Infrarot`, and spectral sensors
- Built for surveying, transport, technical inspection, and advanced field operations

## High-Value Proof Points
- Up to `35 min` flight time with `7.5 kg` payload
- Up to `10 kg` payload in the right category
- Up to `2,000` battery charge cycles
- Modular design for transport, servicing, and fast deployment
- Made-in-Germany framing with in-house battery technology

## Messaging Opportunities For The Redesign
- Reframe the site around premium capability and operational outcomes, not just feature lists
- Shift from generic brochure copy to high-trust technical storytelling
- Use stronger B2B value framing: uptime, deployment speed, flexibility, data quality, compliance, and service
- Add clearer conversion language for enterprise buyers evaluating fit, cost model, and procurement readiness
